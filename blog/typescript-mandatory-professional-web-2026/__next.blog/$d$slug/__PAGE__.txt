<p>Types tell the next developer (or your future self) exactly what a function expects and returns. No more guessing the shape of an API response.</p>
<h2>2. Catching Bugs Before Runtime</h2>
<p>TypeScript catches <code>null</code> pointer exceptions and misspelled properties during development, saving hours of debugging in production.</p>

<p>With tools like <strong>tRPC</strong> or <strong>Prisma</strong>, you can have types that flow from your database all the way to your frontend, ensuring your stack is perfectly aligned.</p>
