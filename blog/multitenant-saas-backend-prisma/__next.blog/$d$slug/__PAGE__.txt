6:Te7b,<p>Multi-tenancy is the ability to serve multiple customers (tenants) from a single database while keeping their data strictly isolated.</p>
<h2>1. The Strategy: Column-based Isolation</h2>
<p>Every table in your database includes a <code>tenantId</code> column. This is the most cost-effective way to scale.</p>
<h2>2. Prisma Middleware</h2>
<p>Use Prisma middleware to automatically inject the <code>tenantId</code> into every query, preventing one customer from accidentally seeing another's data.</p>
