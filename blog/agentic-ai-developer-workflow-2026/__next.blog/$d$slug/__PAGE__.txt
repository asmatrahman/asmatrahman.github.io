6:T452,<p>In 2026, the term "developer" is being redefined. We are no longer just writing syntax; we are architects of autonomous agent systems.</p>
<h2>What is an Agent?</h2>
<p>An AI agent is an LLM wrapped in a loop with access to <strong>tools</strong> (browsers, shells, APIs). It doesn't just suggest code; it tries to run the project, sees the error, and fixes it.</p>
<h2>The New Workflow:</h2>
<ol>
<li><strong>Objective Setting</strong>: Define high-level goals for the agent.</li>
<li><strong>Tool Provisioning</strong>: Give the agent access to the necessary environments.</li>
<li><strong>Supervision</strong>: Review the agent's multi-step "plan" and execution.</li>
<li><strong>Verification</strong>: Test the final output against requirements.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Impact on Productivity</h2>
<p>Traditional coding tasks that took days now take minutes. This allows engineers to focus on <strong>high-level design</strong>, <strong>system architecture</strong>, and <strong>security</strong>.</p>
