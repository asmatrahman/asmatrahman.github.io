<h2>2. The Power of Plugins</h2>
<p>Use <code>@tailwindcss/typography</code> and <code>@tailwindcss/forms</code> to get consistent base styles with zero effort.</p>
<h2>3. Abstracting Components</h2>
<p>If you find yourself repeating classes, move them to a component (React, Vue, or blade). Avoid using <code>@apply</code> unless absolutely necessary, as it decouples your styles from the HTML.</p>
<h2>4. Class Variance Authority (CVA)</h2>
<p>For complex components like buttons with multiple variants, use CVA to manage combinations cleanly.</p>
