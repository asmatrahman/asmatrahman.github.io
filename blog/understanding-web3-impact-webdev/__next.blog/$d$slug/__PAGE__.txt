<h2>1. Decentralized Identity (DID)</h2>
<p>Imagine logging into every site with a single, secure identity that you own, without relying on Google or Facebook. This is the promise of DIDs.</p>
<h2>2. Smart Contracts for Automation</h2>
<p>Using Ethereum or Solana to automate complex, multi-party agreements (like escrow or royalties) without an expensive middleman.</p>

<p>Storing data across a peer-to-peer network to ensure it remains available even if a single server or company goes down.</p>

<p>As a web developer, you don't need to become a "Blockchain Engineer." You just need to know how to connect your React app to these decentralized protocols using libraries like <strong>wagmi</strong> or <strong>ethers.js</strong>.</p>
