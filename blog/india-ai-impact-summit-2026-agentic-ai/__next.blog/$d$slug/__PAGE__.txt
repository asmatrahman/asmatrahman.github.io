6:T26e7,<p>The Delhi sun rose today over the Bharat Mandapam, not just on a new week, but on what many are calling the "Agentic Era." The <strong>India AI Impact Summit 2026</strong> has officially kicked off, and the energy in New Delhi is electric. With giants like Sam Altman, Sundar Pichai, and Jensen Huang in attendance, the message is clear: the age of chatbots is evolving into the age of autonomous agents.</p>
<h2>From Chatbots to Agents: The Great Shift</h2>
<p>For the past two years, we've lived in the era of "Generative AI"—using models to write code, generate images, and answer questions. But as highlighted by Nvidia's Jensen Huang in his opening keynote, we are moving toward <strong>Agentic AI</strong>.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>"The value is no longer in the answer; it's in the action. We aren't building models that talk; we're building models that <em>do</em>." — Jensen Huang</p>
</blockquote>
<h3>What Exactly is Agentic AI?</h3>
<p>Unlike a standard LLM that waits for a prompt, Agentic AI systems are designed to:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Reason</strong>: Break down complex goals into sub-tasks.</li>
<li><strong>Tool-Use</strong>: Proactively use APIs, browse the web, and execute code.</li>
<li><strong>Self-Correct</strong>: Monitor their own progress and try different approaches if they fail.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Key Announcements from Day 1</h2>
<h3>1. OpenAI's "Operator" Global Rollout</h3>
<p>Sam Altman confirmed that <strong>Operator</strong>, OpenAI's autonomous agent framework, is moving out of beta. Operator can navigate a computer interface just like a human, booking flights, managing complex project boards, and even debugging local development environments.</p>
<h3>2. Google's "Project Astra" Integration</h3>
<p>Google announced that Astra is now deeply integrated into the Android kernel. This means your smartphone can now "see" and "do" in real-time, acting as a sentient assistant that understands your physical and digital surroundings simultaneously.</p>
<h3>3. India's "AI Stack" Initiative</h3>
<p>The Government of India unveiled the <strong>India AI Stack</strong>, a set of open-source protocols designed to make autonomous agents interoperable. Much like UPI revolutionized payments, the AI Stack aims to democratize access to sovereign AI infrastructure.</p>
