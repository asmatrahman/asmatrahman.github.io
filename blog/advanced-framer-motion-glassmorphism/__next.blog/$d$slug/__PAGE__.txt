6:Tf94,<p>In the competitive world of SaaS, aesthetics are a feature, not just a preference. Glassmorphism, combined with smooth animations, creates a premium, futuristic feel.</p>
<h2>The Glassmorphism Formula:</h2>
<ol>
<li><strong>Translucency</strong>: Use <code>backdrop-filter: blur(10px)</code>.</li>
<li><strong>Thin Borders</strong>: A subtle white border at low opacity.</li>
<li><strong>Vibrant Backgrounds</strong>: Bright, colorful shapes behind the glass elements.</li>
</ol>
